Tomato Soup

Description

This savory and creamy soup is perfect for a crisp fall day! It is healthy and full of essential vitamins, such as vitamin C, and minerals. It also fits the requirements outlined in the ketogenic diet by providing lots of healthy fats. Pair it with you favorite Italian cheese blend for a high-protein keto meal!

Ingredients

  • 16 ounces crushed tomatoes
  • 4 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 3 Garlic cloves
  • 1 small yellow onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 cups chicken or bone broth
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • Italian seasoning to taste

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot on medium-high heat. Add onions to the pot and stir for 2-3 minutes, then add the garlic and stir the mixture for 1 minute.
  2. Stir in the tomato paste and coat the onion and garlic mixture
  3. Pour in the broth, tomatoes, salt, pepper, and heavy cream, and simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Blend the mixture in a blender on high-speed until smooth. Add seasonings to taste. Garnish with fresh basil or parsley for an aesthetic presentation.
