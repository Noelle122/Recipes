Pork Chops

Description

This simple meal is perfect for a quick dinner after a long day. Despite its simplicity, this dish is flavorful, combining butter, beef stock, and Worcestershire sauce for a rich savory taste. It is high protein and keto-friendly, making it suitable for those with diet sensitivities.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 medium onion (sliced)
  • 2 cups kale (roughly chopped)
  • 2 boneless pork chops
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup beef stock (or beef broth)
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon arrowroot powder
  • 1/3 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup parsley (roughly chopped)

Steps

  1. Remove Instant Pot lid and press SAUTE +10 minutes. Add butter and onions, cook for 3-4 minutes. Add salt and pepper to pork chops. Push onions to one side and add chops to the bottom of the Instant Pot.
  2. Once the pork chops are browned on both sides, add beef stock and Worcestershire sauce. Stir liquid well.
  3. Replace lid close Instant Pot valve. Press MANUAL +8 minutes. When the timer goes off, let Instant Pot naturally release pressure.
  4. Remove pork chops and place on a plate. Stir in arrowroot and sour cream.
  5. Pour in sauce and reduce heat to low-medium. Add butter and let simmer for 10-15 minutes until sauce has thickened and chicken is cooked through.
  6. Spoon cream sauce and onions onto pork chops to serve.
