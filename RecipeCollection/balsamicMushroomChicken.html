Balsamic Mushroom Chicken

Description

This hearty chicken dish makes the perfect dinner! It combines the sweet tanginess of balsamic glaze with the rich umami flavors of mushrooms. It is also convenient to make, and can be stored, adding to its versatility. It is keto-approved, and is suitable for those with diet sensitivities. The high protein content of this dish is also an added bonus!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound chicken thighs or chicken breasts
  • 1 small onion
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 8 ounces bella mushrooms (sliced)
  • 2 tablespoons coconut aminos
  • 1/2 tablespoon monk fruit
  • 1 teaspoon dijon mustard
  • 3 cloves garlic (roughly chopped)
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Make sauce by adding balsamic vinegar, coconut aminos, monk fruit, mustard, 1/2 teaspoon salt, and a pinch of pepper to a small bowl or measuring cup. Whisk well to combine. Set aside.
  2. Pour olive oil to a large skillet set over medium-high heat. Add onions, thyme, and garlic. Cook for 2-3 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add chicken to the pan and cook on each side for 3-4 minutes per side until golden brown.
